She prayed forgiveness the nex t moment, and was sorry; but the first was the emotion of her heart.  “What the half -drunken woman whom I told you of last night, said to me, when I tried to see him and obtain a week’s delay; and what I thought was a mere excuse to avoid me; turns out to have been quite true. He was not only very ill, but dying, then.”  “To whom will our debt be transferred?”  “I don’t know. But before that time we shall be ready with the money; and even though we were not, it would be a bad fortune indeed to find so merciles s a creditor in his successor. We may sleep to -night with light hearts, Caroline!”  Yes. Soften it as they would, their hearts were lighter. The children’s faces, hushed and clustered round to hear what they so little understood, were brighter; and it was a  happier house for this man’s death!
